Trust-Based Shopping Recommander


By: Nizar BELMAHFOUD & Mohammed Amine EL GHARBI

During our studies at Telecom Saint-Etienne, we were conducted to do this application as project in the last year of the engineering cycle.

This application is done to help people to buy products from internet, the users can ask their friends about those products before they buy them.

This is a P2P application developed on the Social Network FACEBOOK, because it's the best way to have informations from a good friends.

And we hope that you will like our application, enjoy it!!
